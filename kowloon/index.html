    • <60s(ROKKON)：31～60秒の作品。
    • <90s(KOWLOON)：61～90秒の作品。

    • #RANDOM分岐あったら最長でも制限以内であること！

  • 「1名」とは、書いてあるBMS製作者の名義だけにより成立される。
    • いずれの偽名でも全く別人として扱われます。
    • 正体が明白であろうか何人であろうか問わず。□□と□□2だって別人だもん。

  • this is a "short bms" event.
  • registration: 2026-06-13(Sat) 00:00:00(UTC+9) ~ 06-21(Sat) 23:59:59(UTC+9).
    impressions: 2026-06-22(Sun) 00:00:00(UTC+9) ~ 07-13(Mon) 23:59:59(UTC+9).
  • in this event, there are three enterable team "categories":
    • <30s(TRIAD): entries must be 0-30 seconds.
    • <60s(ROKKON): entries must be 31-60 seconds.
    • <90s(KOWLOON): entries must be 61-90 seconds.
  • no limit on PR, and songs can be preexisting. the BMS itself must stay unpublished until registration though!
  • the length of an entry (if you're unsure) can be judged as its length as displayed in mBMplay.
    • (in the case of bmson, it is its length displayed during song select in beatoraja-family players.)
    • (more strictly, it is "time between first and last object" for keysounds)
    • if you've got #RANDOM branches, ensure the longest possible outcome is within the limit!
  • an artist can register one entry in each category. however,
  • an "artist" is defined solely by the name of the BMS creator supplied.
    • any aliases will be treated as completely separate people.
    • obvious masking or number of persons is not relevant. even □□ and □□2 are different people.
  • if excess registrations under one name go unfixed for long enough, the excess will have to be disqualified.
    • i.e. fix it and you'll be golden (change the artist name to an alias).
  • when leaving an impression, you can give 0.5pts~1.5pts in 0.1pt intervals. (comments do not affect score.)
    • possible scores: [0.5, 0.6, 0.7, 0.8, 0.9, 1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4, 1.5]
    • overall results will order by mean of the middle 95% of scores. total and median results will be announced too.
  • otherwise, brs003 applies. however,
    • for this event, "BMS Format" forbidden items clauses 3 and 4 will be struck out.
    • (BUT you still bear all responsibility for what you make! don't make ○○ or △△ come and ×× you!)
